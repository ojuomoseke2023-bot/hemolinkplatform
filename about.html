Our Mission

HemoLink bridges the gap between critical blood shortages and life-saving donors. By leveraging automated location-based matching algorithms and precise blood compatibility models, we reduce critical delays from hours to minutes.

Frequently Answered Queries

Healthy individuals aged 18-65, weighing over 50kg, and meeting standard physiological medical criteria can register on the network.
Whole blood donations require a minimum 12-week rest window for males and a 16-week window for females to ensure proper red cell regeneration.
